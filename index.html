Biography

At only 20 years of age, Sangeeta has already established herself as one of the successful artist of the past half a decade.

Born on September 11, 2000, in Allahabad, India, Sangeeta rose to stardom as part of hitting No. 1 with the single ablums and countless mesmerizing concerts round the towns in Uttar Pradesh. Sangeeta had established a solo career of her own as well, havingchart-toppers. Sangeeta also found more music success with rock-oriented tracks and a global dance hit.
